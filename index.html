About Me

I am Ruotian Peng (彭若天), a Ph.D. student at Zhejiang University and Westlake University, advised by Prof. Yandong Wen. I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science from South China University of Technology. I have also spent time at Huawei Foundation Model Lab.

My research focuses on large language models, with a broad interest in improving their reasoning abilities for challenging problems. I am particularly interested in enabling models to explore more effectively through reinforcement learning (SimKO, MOSA), improving formal reasoning so that model outputs become more reliable and verifiable (FormalMATH, T-RL), and studying continual learning in real-world settings where models must adapt without forgetting (PEFT-Arena). Previously, I also worked on multimodal generation (Patch Matters, OpenStory++).

I am currently interested in agent RL and tool-use RL. I believe that language models can develop stronger capabilities by interacting with external environments, tools, and feedback, rather than learning only from static data.
